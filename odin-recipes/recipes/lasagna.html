Description

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh Herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat: cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned.
  2. Cook the noodles: boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water.
  3. Make the cheese layer: mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, salt and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasagna: Layer the ingredients, start with sauce and end with mozzarella.
  5. Bake the lasagna: cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for half an hour.
